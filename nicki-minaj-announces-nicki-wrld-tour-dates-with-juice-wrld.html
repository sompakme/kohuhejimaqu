Nicki Minaj Announces &quot;Nicki Wrld&quot; Tour Dates With Juice WRLD

September 07, 2024
blog

Back in the summer, Nicki Minaj announced that she would be heading on tour with Future and a slew of impressive opening acts to promote her newest album. Before she even got a chance to reveal all of the faces appearing on the card, the North American tour dates were postponed and Future was no longer set to be involved in the show. Nicki's European fans will still be getting a show next year as the Queen has just announced a new name for her world tour, inviting along one of the hottest up-and-coming stars in the industry.

"Lucid Dreams" has been one of the most successful songs this year, raking in tons of streams for Juice WRLD as he continues to impress with each offering. His next endeavor will be a European tour with Nicki Minaj as the New York rapper has just announced that she'll be taking Juice under her wing on the Nicki Wrld Tour. 

The shows will kick off in February as it will run for twenty-two nights. The duo will be spending a month overseas in Germany, France, Sweden, and other countries. According to Billboard, Nicki will be encouraging local acts to open up for her, giving a unique opportunity to European rappers that may not have another chance to work with Minaj. 

The artist issued a statement, saying, "I’m so excited to bring the full Nicki Minaj experience to my fans in Europe. I have been carefully crafting the most exhilarating, unique, memorable piece of art to share with my incredible fans. I’m in love with Juice Wrld’s music & feel so stoked to have him join me. This will be one for the books."

Are you looking forward to it?
