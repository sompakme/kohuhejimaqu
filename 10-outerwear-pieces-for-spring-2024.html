10 Outerwear Pieces for Spring 2024

August 03, 2024
blog

BURBERRY Long Gabardine Trench Coat

BURBERRY Long Gabardine Trench Coat

Burberry’s Gabardine trench coat is crafted with water-resistant fabric that will get you through the worst of April showers. This timeless piece is also extremely versatile, so you’ll have endless outfit formulas for the upcoming spring season.

PROENZA SCHOULER WHITE LABEL Amalia Blazer

PROENZA SCHOULER WHITE LABEL Amalia Blazer

During the early portion of spring, there are often unpredictable days that will tease you with breezy temperatures in the afternoon and surprise you with gusts of wind in the evening. For moments like this, you’ll need Proenza Schouler’s wool-blend blazer.

SACAI Chalk Stripe x Suiting Bonding Jacket

SACAI Chalk Stripe x Suiting Bonding Jacket

We know black isn't a main element of spring's typical color palette, but we firmly believe that you should have at least one black lightweight jacket for practical purposes. Before you argue with us, take a look at Sacai’s belted blazer, which features a form fitting silhouette that will pair nicely with a range of looks.

SERGIO HUDSON Wool and Cashmere-Blend Blazer

SERGIO HUDSON Wool and Cashmere-Blend Blazer

Sergio Hudson is always on point regarding the latest trends in fashion, so it comes as no surprise that he has chosen to experiment with burgundy. With this piece, we can assume that Hudson kept the silhouette simple to let the color truly shine.

The Row June Cotton Trench Coat

The Row June Cotton Trench Coat

The Row’s June Cotton trench coat is a wardrobe mainstay that will last for years to come. This traditional coat is lined in silk for an extra lavish appeal.

AMI Double Breasted Oversized Jacket

AMI Double Breasted Oversized Jacket

Ami’s double breasted jacket features a subtle houndstooth pattern that we absolutely love. To make this pattern pop, consider wearing this piece above a fitted black t-shirt.

ALEXANDER WANG Wrap Tie Blazer Black and Brown

ALEXANDER WANG Wrap Tie Blazer Black and Brown

Apparently shoulder pads are making a comeback, and we’re fully endorsing this vintage style. Alexander Wang’s wrap tie blazer perfectly masters the concept of proportion play by balancing structured shoulders with a flexible wrap tie that cinches the waist.

DEREK LAM Milo Double Breasted Crop Jacket

DEREK LAM Milo Double Breasted Crop Jacket

If you’re seeking a clean look that pays homage to minimalism, style Derek Lam’s sage-colored cropped jacket with beige high waisted trousers and a pair of neutral heels. To complete the look, you'll need understated gold jewelry and a small handbag.

AMI Double Breasted Oversized Jacket in Powder Pink

AMI Double Breasted Oversized Jacket in Powder Pink

It seems as though fashion experts are leaving vibrant shades of Barbie pink in the past. Instead, they’re opting for softer hues like powder pink. This lovely piece offers the perfect hint of color without being too overwhelming.

DONNI The Quilted Jacket

DONNI The Quilted Jacket

Donni’s quilted jacket features an extra classy touch with a charming mother of pearl button placed right at the opening. On the jacket itself, you'll see stitched lines that create a unique rippled effect.
