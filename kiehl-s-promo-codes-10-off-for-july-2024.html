FAQs

Does Kiehl’s have free shipping?

Yes, Kiehl's offers free shipping in the US on all orders more than $50 which will arrive on your doorstep within 3-5 business days. Orders below $50 will cost $8 for standard shipping. Make sure to check this Marie Claire page for the occasional free shipping promo codes.  

How can I get 15% off my first order at Kiehl's?

You can get a Kiehl’s discount on your first order by signing up for the Kiehl’s newsletter with your preferred email address. Simply click the relevant code on this page. Alternatively, visit Kiehl’s website, scroll to the footer, and type your email address. Once you’ve signed up, Kiehl’s will send you 15% off your next order.  

Does Kiehl’s have student discount?

Kiehl’s does not have a student discount available currently, though they have in the past. Our team regularly checks the web for new promotions so we’ll be sure to let you know if this changes in the future. Students can still save on their Kiehl’s skincare by signing up for emails or shopping the ‘Offers’ page. 

When is Kiehl's biggest sale?

Kiehl’s has sales throughout the year, often around the holidays or key sales dates. In the past, we've seen discounts of up to 30% off skincare products for Black Friday. As there is no dedicated Kiehl’s sales page on their website, it’s worth signing up for emails to be kept updated. Kiehl sale products tend to sell out quickly, so don't miss out! 

How can I get free samples at Kiehl’s?

Once you’ve added your Kiehl’s skincare to your basket, click ‘Checkout’. You can then choose 3 free samples to go with your order. As of writing, current samples include the Ultra Face Cream, Avocado Eye Cream Treatment, and Anti-Ageing Retinol. Simply add these to your bag at the checkout page, and continue to payment. 

What is the return policy at Kiehl’s?

Kiehl’s has 100% back money-guarantee on all of their products except for products marked as final sale, promo gift items, or e-gift cards. You can return your order for free to a Kiehl’s location or by mail for a return fee. To start your return, go to the Kiehl’s Return page on their website.
