Blue Ivy turns five on Saturday! Check out her best moments so far

September 12, 2024
blog

Beyonce and Jay-Z's eldest daughter turns five on Saturday

    She had her own website

    After her birth, Jay-Z and Beyonce set up a website just for Blue Ivy to share snaps of their newborn baby. Alongside photos of themselves with baby Blue, the proud parents wrote: "We welcome you to share in our joy. Thank you for respecting our privacy during this beautiful time in our lives."

    ©The Carter Family

    She wore a £8,300 dress to the VMAs

    Little Blue Ivy made her red carpet debut with her mum at the MTV VMA music awards in August 2016. She dressed in a glittery gown with a long tutu train, ornately embellished bodice and flowery waist, which was worth an incredible $11k (£8,300), made by children's designer Mischka Aoki at Bergdorf Goodman.

    She joined her mum on stage

    Jay-Z took Blue Ivy on stage to congratulate her mum after her performance at the 2014 MTV Video Music Awards. Beyonce looked visibly moved as she smiled at her daughter, who was also spotted dancing along to her mum's performance, much to the amusement of her dad.

    She became a big sister

    Blue Ivy was delighted to get a little brother and sister in 2017 after Beyonce gave birth to twins. Blue's grandmother, Tina Knowles, opened up to about how she has taken to her role as a big sister, explaining: "She's very proud and excited. She's a good big sister, she really is. She cares for them a lot."

    She's travelled the world

    Blue Ivy has already travelled around the world, and celebrated her third birthday in Thailand. She also went on tour with the singer when she was just 19-months-old. Speaking about taking her on the trip, Beyonce said: "It's really interesting because I'm touring with my daughter for the first time and my dream has always been to be able to balance my life and my career. I think it's the one thing that women struggle with because it's so difficult."

    Her name is trademarked

    Not all five-year-olds have their names trademarked, but Blue Ivy is the exception! Jay-Z opened up about their decision to trademark her name, explaining to : "People wanted to make products based on our child's name and you don't want anybody trying to benefit off your baby's name. It wasn't for us to do anything; as you see, we haven't done anything."

    She starred in her mum's music video

    Blue starred in her mum's music video for Dressed in a white frilly dress, the little girl played with her friends and giggled to the camera in the video, and looked the spitting image of her famous mum! She also most recently starred in her dad's music video for where a grown-up version of her becomes President and is surrounded by inspirational women including Mindy Kaling and Rosario Dawson.

    She's already a recording artist

    The internet went wild when it was revealed that Blue Ivy freestyled in a song called on Jay-Z's 4:44 album. She rapped: "Never seen a ceiling in my whole life.. Boom shakalaka, boom shakalaka. Everything in shaka, everything in faka." She also had a credit in her dad's song as she can be heard crying on the track, which was released just two days after her birth.
