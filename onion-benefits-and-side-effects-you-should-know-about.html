Onions contain vitamins C and A, flavonoids, sulphur, phytochemicals and are a source of other useful elements. Onions are rich in minerals, potassium, iron, useful chemicals, and fiber.   • Healthier heart

Different studies have shown that people who consume onions on a regular basis can potentially reduce extra high blood pressure, prevent blood platelets, make blood thinner and reduce the risks of heart attack. In other words, this vegetable can improve your heart health, and we all know how important this organ is in our lives.

Onion side effects

Excess amounts of any product can lead to disadvantages for your health. This means that people who consume too many onions might experience side effects instead of benefits of onions in the body.

  • Allergy source

Those people who are allergic to different products need to be sure they are not allergic to onion before consuming it. Allergy can lead to dangerous consequences, so it is not safe to eat this vegetable if you have an allergy.

  • Heartburn issue

Onions are not recommended in big quantities to pregnant women and those people who suffer from heartburn problems in their regular life. Experiencing the unpleasant ‘burning’ feeling in the chest is horrible, that is why you should treat your stomach with healthy foods and not just raw onions.
