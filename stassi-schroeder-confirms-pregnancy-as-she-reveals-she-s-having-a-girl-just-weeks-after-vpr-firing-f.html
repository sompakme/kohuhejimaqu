FIRED Vanderpump Rules star Stassi Schroeder has confirmed she's expecting a baby with boyfriend Beau Clark.

The 31-year-old revealed did a gender reveal post on Instagram on Tuesday, as she announced her happy news.

“We’re having a baby girl,” she captioned a photo, which showed Clark kissing her cheek while holding a pink onesie with the hashtag #OOTD.

Schroeder lovingly cradled her bump as she looked blossoming in a dusky pink bardot sweater dress.

Her bump had been noted in photos but she hadn't personally addressed the rumor.

It comes less than a week after it was announced that she was fired from Vanderpump Rules.

Bravo announced a week ago that she would not be returning to the reality series following claims she had exhibited racist behavior.

The network fired both Stassi and co-star Kristen Doute for their behavior against Faith Stowers.

Faith claimed Stassi and Kristen had made a false claim to cops about her in 2017.

Stassi apologized on Instagram writing: "Racially insensitive comments from my past have resurfaced. It is important that I continue to take accountability for what I have said and done while pushing myself to do better.

“I have grown significantly from the person I was then, and I am still filled with remorse and regret for the hurt I caused.
