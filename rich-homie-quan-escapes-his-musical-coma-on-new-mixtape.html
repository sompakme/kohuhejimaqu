Rich Homie Quan Escapes His Musical &quot;Coma&quot; On New Mixtape

August 03, 2024

Rich Homie Quan was on pace to enjoy an illustrious career in the rap game and, although he can consider himself a success story, his partner Young Thug ended up getting the shine that they both deserved. Somewhere along the way, the people forgot about RHQ but he's still out here releasing solid sounds, putting on for Atlanta and representing his unique style. It's been a relatively quiet year for the 30-year-old rapper but today, he returns with a brand new mixtape called Coma. 

All week, Quan has been teasing the arrival of his new body of work, announcing that the 10-track display would be on pace to drop on December 13.
