But let’s start from the beginning, because every good shoe has a starting story, too. Not to mention, it’s amassed an impressive list of A-list fans that includes (but is not limited to) Gigi Hadid, Zoe Saldaña, Camila Cabello, and Lucy Hale. I’ve kept a very close eye on it, so you can trust me when I say that $100 off is a steal, and it’s no surprise that sizes are selling out lightning fast. The one downside? You only have a few days to get your hands on these savings, so snag your pair now. No hesitation allowed, but if you need more convincing, allow me to help.

Comfort is certainly not lacking, either, thanks to the padded insole and upper that ensures no pesky rubbing against the top of your foot. Genius!
