Paris Hilton and Kim Kardashian Had a Reunion on Christmas Eve

July 14, 2024
blog

Christmas is a time for getting together with your loved ones, relatives, friends... and even frenemies and employees you had organize your closet in the early 2000s. In other words, it was the perfect time for a long-awaited Paris Hilton-Kim Kardashian reunion.

In a turn of events bound to transport you to 2006, Hilton shared a photo of herself and Kim from the Kardashian's extravagant Christmas Eve party. "Lovely evening celebrating the holidays with @KimKardashian," she captioned the shot. (Sadly, there were no Juicy Couture track suits included.)

According to People, this marks the first time the duo was photographed since they reunited at Riccardo Tisci's birthday party in Ibiza in 2014.

Since the photo is giving us major '00s nostalgia, we can't help but fondly look back at the simpler, happier times of oversized sunglasses, velour separates and T-Mobile Sidekicks.
