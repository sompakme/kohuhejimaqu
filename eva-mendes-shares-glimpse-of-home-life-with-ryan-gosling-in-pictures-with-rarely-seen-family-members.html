Eva Mendes shares glimpse of home life with Ryan Gosling in pictures with rarely-seen family members

August 26, 2024

The Hitch actress shared photos from her Easter Sunday

    Eva Mendes and Ryan Gosling may be keen to keep their personal life out of the public eye, but the former is an avid social media poster, and every now and then fans get a glimpse of their life at home.

    Such was the case over the weekend, when the actress and her family celebrated Easter Sunday.

    Eva and Ryan have two daughters together, Esmeralda Amada, 8, and Amada Lee, 6. Though neither have ever confirmed that they have married – the mom-of-two will however occasionally call Ryan her husband – they have been together since 2011.

    The slew of snapshots see her taking on a variety of roles, from vintage truck driver, to dog lover, and even unicorn sculpture owner, all of which she listed in her caption.
