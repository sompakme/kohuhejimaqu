Early Life and Education

Tim Gunn was born on July 29, 1953, in Washington, D.C. He attended the Corcoran College of Art and Design, where he received a BFA in Sculpture. He later earned his MFA from the School of the Arts Institute of Chicago.

Project Runway

Tim Gunn’s Guide to Style
