The Wild Twins from Naked and Afraid are still exploring the world

September 10, 2024
blog
Spread the love

“Naked and Afraid,” the Discovery show that challenges participants to survive in the wild, stark naked, for 21 days, has intrigued fans for years. Curious about the fate of the adventurous twins who braved the show? In this 2022 update, we’ll explore what’s been happening in the lives of the ‘Wild Twins’ since their unforgettable appearance on the show.

Surviving the South African Savannah

The show’s premise required survivalists to construct shelter, forage for food, and steer clear of the dangers posed by hyenas and leopards while inhabiting the South African Savannah. Now, let’s delve into the whereabouts of these intrepid stars of “Naked and Afraid” and discover what they’ve been up to since their Discovery Channel debut.

Meet the Twins of Adventure

Back in February 2020, the Savannah became home to two sets of twins: Amber and Serena Shine, and Warren and Thomas Virget. These courageous twins were tasked with the challenge of enduring 21 days in the wild.Season 11, episode 8, titled ‘Twinning,’ showcased the incredible journey of these four survivalists. Their mission: to protect themselves from the diverse array of wild African animals, including rhinos, leopards, and hyenas.

Amber and Serena Shine: The Wild Twins

Amber and Serena Shine, known as The Wild Twins on Instagram, hail from the picturesque town of Waiuku in New Zealand. In 2020, during an interview with Discovery Australia, they revealed that they were approached by the Discovery team to participate in “Naked and Afraid” because of their passion for survivalism, a passion that was evident on their Facebook page.The sisters described the experience as an “awesome challenge,” but one they wouldn’t necessarily recommend to everyone. It takes a special kind of adventurer to brave the wild with nothing but nature as their companion.

Tracking the Wild Twins in 2022

Keeping up with The Wild Twins in 2022 is a breeze, thanks to their active presence on social media. They have garnered quite the following, with 28K followers on Facebook and an impressive 25.7K followers on Instagram. To stay updated on their thrilling adventures, you can find them on IG @the_wild_twins.Their Instagram bio sums it up perfectly: they’re “wild, crazy, and adventurous.” In 2022, Serena and Amber added a new feather to their cap by publishing their own book, titled “The Wild Twins: Tales of Strength and Survival.” The book promises to be a thrilling read, sharing their incredible journey and the lessons they’ve learned along the way.As you scroll through their Instagram feed, you’ll find captivating snapshots of their travels, from the enchanting landscapes of Bolivia and Peru to the rugged beauty of South Dakota. The Wild Twins continue to inspire adventure enthusiasts and armchair travelers alike with their indomitable spirit and zest for life.So, as 2022 unfolds, The Wild Twins show no signs of slowing down. Their thirst for adventure and their dedication to survivalism keep them exploring and sharing their extraordinary experiences with the world.
