Air Jordan 37 Low &quot;EYBL&quot; Gets Official Images

August 29, 2024
blog

Overall, the Air Jordan 37 Low has proven to be a worthy companion piece to the Air Jordan 37. Although they look the same, there is a key difference when it comes to the silhouette. Of course, the fact that the Low is a low-top sneaker is the defining aspect. As obvious as this is, there are some who question why these variations exist. However, the reasoning is quite simple. Some hoopers want something that is lower to the ground. It is a simple matter of taste that Jordan Brand is more than happy to oblige.

Subsequently, we have been seeing some awesome new colorways of the Air Jordan 37 Low. Although the Air Jordan 38 is coming soon, it is time to get in as many offerings of this current silhouette as possible. There are plenty of great combinations that Jumpman can go with here. In fact, the latest Jordan 37 Low to be unveiled seemingly has a vibrant summer aesthetic. With the Elite Youth Basketball League in full swing, an "EYBL" color scheme is now on the horizon.

"EYBL" Air Jordan 37 Low

Above and below, you can see some of the official images. Firstly, the upper is covered in a nice orange glow. From there, we get lots of green coming through the outsole before spreading to the tongue and the shoe's inner lining. From there, we have an interesting midsole that has a pinkish glow to it, mixed with orange speckles. Overall, this makes for a very colorful and loud color scheme.

More Photos

For now, this is a sneaker without a release date. However, we do know that the price attached to these is $195 USD. Overall, this is going to be a loud shoe that can definitely make some noise on the court this summer. Hopefully, more great Air Jordan 37 Low colorways are on the horizon.
