Valley Isle Community FCU now open

The Valley Isle Community Federal Credit Union opened a Wailuku branch at 69 North Market Street.

The new location will be open from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Monday through Friday. “Our new location will help us enhance our ability to serve our community and further our mission to help members live better lives every, single, day,” said Isaac Pena-Parrilla, FCU Development Manager. “We are ecstatic about this opportunity and honored to be a part of the Wailuku Town economic revitalization plan.” For more information, visit www.vicfcu.org.

Chick-fil-A Kahului seeking employees

Chick-fil-A Kahului is seeking more than 100 part-time and full-time team members for its Kahului restaurant near Target.

From 10 a.m to 3 p.m. Tuesday through Friday, applicants may stop by the restaurant to pick up an application and will have the opportunity to interview on-site.

“We’re excited to welcome over 100 Team Members to Chick-fil-A Kahului, the very first Chick-fil-A on Maui,” said Sean Whaley, local franchise operator of Chick-fil-A Kahului. “We look forward to building a team focused on creating a welcoming environment for our guests and making a positive impact on the local community.”

The restaurants offer its employees benefits along with leadership opportunities, college scholarships and hands-on training and mentoring by the local restaurant operator, according to an announcement.

Interested applicants may also see workstream.us/j/mg_partners_inc_chickfila.

Lahaina Theater features surf films

The newly remodeled Regency Theatres Wharf Cinemas will feature classic and thrilling surf films back on the big screen Wednesday nights at 7 p.m. starting this week through August 3.

Presented in partnership with Hurley in Lahaina, each Wednesday night will feature surf films, fun and prizes, beginning with “Endless Summer” followed by “Riding Giants” on July 13.

“There is something very special about watching these cinematic masterpieces of surf culture on the big screen in a communal environment with friends and family” said Lyndon Golin of Regency Theatres.

Tickets cost $8 per seat. The Regency Wharf Cinemas is located at 658 Front Street in Lahaina on the second floor of the Wharf Cinema Center.

Tickets and information are available at www.regencymovies.com.

Company launches ShedPro Hawaii

Truss Systems Hawaii of Maui launched ShedPro Hawaii, the first custom-made sheds company of its kind in the state and county.

Since 2020, the demand for sheds grew as people began needing extra space for their home projects and new ideas. ShedPro Hawaii offers three basic models with a wide variety of paint, doors, windows, and more.

These sheds will outlast plastic shed options sold at most home improvement stores, the company said.

“Our innovative design technology for our custom sheds came at the perfect time when families were confronted with an increasing need for additional storage space.” Sales Manager Zachary Loomer said. “Our sheds also answered the needs for people looking for additional space to their home, such as a “she” or “he-shed,” and we have enjoyed providing quality shed solutions for the community since 2010.

For more information or to online, visit www.shedprohawaii.com.
