Bhad Bhabie Taps Ty Dolla $ign, YG, Lil Baby, Lil Yachty &amp; More For &quot;15&quot; Tracklist

September 25, 2024
blog

Whether you like her or not, Bhad Bhabie isn't going anywhere. In an era where memes influence popularity, she's managed to escape being known as the "Cash Me Ousside" girl and became a certified rapper with charting singles on the Billboard Hot 100. Next week, she'll release her debut mixtape, 15 and it looks like she's brought out some of the hottest in the game for the tracklist.

Bhad Bhabie has just revealed the official tracklist to her debut mixtape, 15. The rapper's forthcoming project will feature the previously released singles, "15 (Intro)," "Trust Me" ft. Ty Dolla $ign, "Gucci Flip Flops" ft. Lil Yachty and "Yung & Bhad" ft. City Girls.  Bhad Bhabie also taps Asian Doll, - who she previously brought on her "Bhanned In America" tour earlier this year - YG and Lil Baby for the tracklist.

With the new project set to drop on September 18th, she's also gearing up to hit the road with Lil Yachty this fall. Bhad Bhabie will be opening up for Lil Yachty on the "Disrespect" tour which is set to kick off on October 24th in Baltimore before concluding in Santa Cruz at the end of November. There's a good chance we'll see the two of them perform their collab "Gucci Flip Flops" throughout the tour.
